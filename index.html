About us

We would like to familiarize you a little with Indian cuisine. Our dishes are fresh daily according to traditional Indian recipes prepared. We use fresh meat (chicken from Germany) and over 42 different authentic spices. All dishes are spiced and digestible by our chef. If you like to eat spicy or medium spicy food, do not hesitate to state this when placing your order.

We also offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes. Ask us and we will be happy to give you more information about the ingredients used in our dishes. Since we prepare all dishes fresh for you, there may be a short wait. We wish you a pleasant stay and above all a good appetite!
